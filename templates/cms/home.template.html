 Shipping Information

Please refer to the postage chart for estimated delivery time for your state. We post your goods as quickly as we can. 99% of all purchases are freighted out of our warehouse within 24 hours.Most larger items are sent with e-Parcel. e-Parcel is an Australia Post parcel delivery service that requires a signature on delivery. If no-one is home at the time of delivery then a card will be left in your letter-box requesting that you pick the parcel up from your local post office.Many smaller items are sent in an envelope with stamps. These items are not tracked and will be left in your mailbox. Please understand that we have no control over the Australia Post mail network. TNT Freight Details
